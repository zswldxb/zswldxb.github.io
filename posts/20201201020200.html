2.2 Multiplying Matrices and Vectors(矩阵和向量相乘)

One of the most important operations involving matrices is multiplication of two matrices. The matrix product of matrices AAA and BBB is a third matrix CCC. In order for this product to be deﬁned, AAA must have the same number of columns as BBB has rows. If AAA is of shape m×nm\times nm×n and BBB is of shape n×pn\times pn×p, then CCC is of shape m×pm\times pm×p. We can write the matrix product just by placing two or more matrices together, for example,

矩阵乘法是矩阵运算中最重要的操作之一。两个矩阵 AAA 和 BBB 的矩阵乘积（matrix product）是第三个矩阵 CCC。为了使乘法可被定义，矩阵 AAA 的列数必须和矩阵 BBB 的行数相等。如果矩阵 AAA 的形状是 m×nm\times nm×n，矩阵 BBB 的形状是 n×pn\times pn×p，那么矩阵 CCC 的形状是 m×pm\times pm×p。我们可以通过将两个或多个矩阵并列放置以书写矩阵乘法，例如

C=AB.(2.4)C=AB.\qquad\qquad\qquad(2.4)C=AB.(2.4)

The product operation is deﬁned by

具体地，该乘法操作定义为

Ci,j=∑kAi,kBk,j.(2.5)C_{i,j}=\sum_{k}A_{i,k}B_{k,j}.\qquad\qquad\qquad(2.5)Ci,j​=k∑​Ai,k​Bk,j​.(2.5)

Note that the standard product of two matrices is not just a matrix containing the product of the individual elements. Such an operation exists and is called the element-wise product, or Hadamard product, and is denoted as A⊙BA\odot BA⊙B.

需要注意的是，两个矩阵的标准乘积不是指两个矩阵中对应元素的乘积。不过，那样的矩阵操作确实是存在的，称为元素对应乘积（element-wise product）或者Hadamard乘积（Hadamard product），记为 A⊙BA\odot BA⊙B。

The dot product between two vectors xxx and yyy of the same dimensionality is the matrix product xTyx^{T}yxTy. We can think of the matrix product C=ABC=ABC=AB as computing Ci,jC_{i,j}Ci,j​ as the dot product between row iii of AAA and column jjj of BBB.

两个相同维数的向量 xxx 和 yyy 的点积（dot product）可看作矩阵乘积。我们可以把矩阵乘积 C=ABC=ABC=AB 中计算 Ci,jC_{i,j}Ci,j​ 的步骤看作 AAA 的第 iii 行和 BBB 的第 jjj 列之间的点积。

Matrix product operations have many useful properties that make mathematical analysis of matrices more convenient. For example, matrix multiplication is distributive:

矩阵乘积运算有许多有用的性质，从而使矩阵的数学分析更加方便。比如，矩阵乘积服从分配律：

A(B+C)=AB+AC.(2.6)A(B+C)=AB+AC.\qquad\qquad\qquad(2.6)A(B+C)=AB+AC.(2.6)

It is also associative:

矩阵乘积也服从结合律：

A(BC)=(AB)C.(2.7)A(BC)=(AB)C.\qquad\qquad\qquad(2.7)A(BC)=(AB)C.(2.7)

Matrix multiplication is not commutative (the condition AB=BAAB=BAAB=BA does not always hold), unlike scalar multiplication. However, the dot product between two vectors is commutative:

不同于标量乘积，矩阵乘积并不满足交换律（AB=BAAB=BAAB=BA 的情况并非总是满足）。然而，两个向量的点积满足交换律：

xTy=yTx.(2.8)x^{T}y = y^{T}x.\qquad\qquad\qquad(2.8)xTy=yTx.(2.8)

The transpose of a matrix product has a simple form:

矩阵乘积的转置有着简单的形式：

(AB)T=BTAT.(2.9)(AB)^{T}= B^{T}A^{T}.\qquad\qquad\qquad(2.9)(AB)T=BTAT.(2.9)

This enables us to demonstrate equation 2.8 by exploiting the fact that the value of such a product is a scalar and therefore equal to its own transpose:

利用两个向量点积的结果是标量、标量转置是自身的事实，我们可以证明式（2.8）：

xTy=(xTy)T=yTx.(2.10)x^{T}y =(x^{T}y)^{T}=y^{T}x.\qquad\qquad\qquad(2.10)xTy=(xTy)T=yTx.(2.10)

Since the focus of this textbook is not linear algebra, we do not attempt to develop a comprehensive list of useful properties of the matrix product here, but the reader should be aware that many more exist.

由于本书的重点不是线性代数，我们并不想展示矩阵乘积的所有重要性质，但读者应该知道矩阵乘积还有很多有用的性质。
