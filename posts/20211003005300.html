Pytorch.torch.nn.BatchNorm1d

BatchNorm1d\text{BatchNorm1d}BatchNorm1d 网络层要求其输入为二维张量 Input∈Rbatch_size×channels\text{Input}\in\R^{\text{batch\_size}\times\text{channels}}Input∈Rbatch_size×channels 或三维张量 Input∈Rbatch_size×channels×features\text{Input}\in\R^{\text{batch\_size}\times\text{channels}\times\text{features}}Input∈Rbatch_size×channels×features. 其将对每个通道维度的所有数据计算均值和方差, 然后归一化, 然后进行仿射变换(参数可学习). 其输出与输入张量形状相同.

这里以三维输入张量 Input∈Rbatch_size×channels×features\text{Input}\in\R^{\text{batch\_size}\times\text{channels}\times\text{features}}Input∈Rbatch_size×channels×features 为例, 其经过 BatchNorm1d\text{BatchNorm1d}BatchNorm1d 后的输出张量为 Output∈Rbatch_size×channels×features\text{Output}\in\R^{\text{batch\_size}\times\text{channels}\times\text{features}}Output∈Rbatch_size×channels×features. 具体操作如下:

  1. 对于每个通道 ch(ch∈N∩[0,channels−1])ch(ch\in\N\cap[0,\text{channels}-1])ch(ch∈N∩[0,channels−1]) 的所有数据计算其均值 μch\mu_{ch}μch​:

μch=∑bsbatch_size−1∑ffeatures−1Input(bs,ch,f)batch_size×features\mu_{ch}=\dfrac{\displaystyle\sum_{bs}^{\text{batch\_size}-1}\sum_{f}^{\text{features}-1}\text{Input}(bs,ch,f)}{\text{batch\_size}\times\text{features}}μch​=batch_size×featuresbs∑batch_size−1​f∑features−1​Input(bs,ch,f)​

  1. 对于每个通道 ch(ch∈N∩[0,channels−1])ch(ch\in\N\cap[0,\text{channels}-1])ch(ch∈N∩[0,channels−1]) 的所有数据计算其方差 σch2\sigma_{ch}^{2}σch2​:

σch2=∑bsbatch_size−1∑ffeatures−1(Input(bs,ch,f)−μch)2batch_size×features\sigma_{ch}^{2}=\dfrac{\displaystyle\sum_{bs}^{\text{batch\_size}-1}\sum_{f}^{\text{features}-1}(\text{Input}(bs,ch,f)-\mu_{ch})^{2}}{\text{batch\_size}\times\text{features}}σch2​=batch_size×featuresbs∑batch_size−1​f∑features−1​(Input(bs,ch,f)−μch​)2​

  1. 对于每个通道 ch(ch∈N∩[0,channels−1])ch(ch\in\N\cap[0,\text{channels}-1])ch(ch∈N∩[0,channels−1]) 的所有数据进行归一化:

Hidden(bs,ch,f)=(Input(bs,ch,f)−μch)σch2+ε\text{Hidden}(bs,ch,f)=\dfrac{(\text{Input}(bs,ch,f)-\mu_{ch})}{\sqrt{\sigma_{ch}^{2}+\varepsilon}}Hidden(bs,ch,f)=σch2​+ε​(Input(bs,ch,f)−μch​)​

  1. 增加放缩和平移变量 γ\gammaγ 和 β\betaβ (可学习的仿射变换参数), 进行仿射变换后为:

Output(bs,ch,f)=γchHidden(bs,ch,f)+βch\text{Output}(bs,ch,f)=\gamma_{ch}\text{Hidden}(bs,ch,f)+\beta_{ch}Output(bs,ch,f)=γch​Hidden(bs,ch,f)+βch​

因此可以得到输出 Output\text{Output}Output.

我们希望在测试过程中, 使用训练数据的均值和方差, 因此在训练过程中分别记录整体样本的均值和方差, 以便在测试时使用. 在训练过程中, 采用动量更新的方式, 设动量 momentum\text{momentum}momentum, 其过程如下:
