Pytorch.torch.nn.UpsamplingBilinear2d

网络层 UpsamplingBilinear2d\text{UpsamplingBilinear2d}UpsamplingBilinear2d 为双线性插值上采样层. 网络层 UpsamplingBilinear2d\text{UpsamplingBilinear2d}UpsamplingBilinear2d 要求其输入 Input\text{Input}Input 为四维(图像数据)张量. 其计算过程对齐输入与输出的四个角.

其重要参数如下:

  • size: 表示输出的特征维度大小(不包括小批量数和通道数).
  • scale_factor: 表示相对于输入特征维度大小的放缩比例.

示例

import torch
import torch.nn as nn

print(f'torch version: {torch.__version__}')
# torch version: 1.10.2

batch_size = 1
in_channels = 2
in_height = 2
in_width = 2

input = torch.arange(batch_size*in_channels*in_height*in_width, dtype=torch.float32).reshape((batch_size, in_channels, in_height, in_width))
print(f'input: {input}')
'''
input: tensor([[[[0., 1.],
          [2., 3.]],

         [[4., 5.],
          [6., 7.]]]])
'''

layer = nn.UpsamplingBilinear2d(scale_factor=2)
y = layer(input)
print(f'output: {y}')
'''
output: tensor([[[[0.0000, 0.3333, 0.6667, 1.0000],
          [0.6667, 1.0000, 1.3333, 1.6667],
          [1.3333, 1.6667, 2.0000, 2.3333],
          [2.0000, 2.3333, 2.6667, 3.0000]],

         [[4.0000, 4.3333, 4.6667, 5.0000],
          [4.6667, 5.0000, 5.3333, 5.6667],
          [5.3333, 5.6667, 6.0000, 6.3333],
          [6.0000, 6.3333, 6.6667, 7.0000]]]])
'''
