信息熵的最大值

下面讨论一下信息熵的最大值，即

max⁡Ent(D)=−∑k=1Npklog⁡2pks.t.∑k=1Npk=1\begin{aligned} &\max&Ent(D) &= -\sum_{k=1}^{N} p_{k}\log_{2}p_{k}\\ &s.t.&\sum_{k=1}^{N}p_{k} &= 1 \end{aligned}​maxs.t.​Ent(D)k=1∑N​pk​​=−k=1∑N​pk​log2​pk​=1​

我们将信息熵的中的符号去掉，即最小化 ∑k=1Npklog⁡2pk\displaystyle{\sum_{k=1}^{N} p_{k}\log_{2}p_{k}}k=1∑N​pk​log2​pk​，我们采用拉格朗日乘子法，设

J(p1,p2,⋯ ,pk,λ)=∑k=1Npklog⁡2pk+λ(∑k=1Npk−1)\begin{aligned} J(p_{1},p_{2},\cdots,p_{k},\lambda)&= \sum_{k=1}^{N} p_{k}\log_{2}p_{k}+\lambda(\sum_{k=1}^{N}p_{k}-1 )\\ \end{aligned}J(p1​,p2​,⋯,pk​,λ)​=k=1∑N​pk​log2​pk​+λ(k=1∑N​pk​−1)​

对 J(p1,p2,⋯ ,pk,λ)J(p_{1},p_{2},\cdots,p_{k},\lambda)J(p1​,p2​,⋯,pk​,λ) 求关于 p1,p2,⋯ ,pk,λp_{1},p_{2},\cdots,p_{k},\lambdap1​,p2​,⋯,pk​,λ 的导数，并令其为 0 ，可得

∂J(p1,p2,⋯ ,pk,λ)∂pk=log⁡2pk+1ln⁡2+λ=0,k=1,2,⋯ ,N∂J(p1,p2,⋯ ,pk,λ)∂λ=∑k=1Npk−1=0\begin{aligned} \frac{\partial J(p_{1},p_{2},\cdots,p_{k},\lambda)}{\partial p_{k}} &= \log_{2}p_{k} + \frac{1}{\ln 2} +\lambda = 0,\quad k =1,2,\cdots,N\\ \frac{\partial J(p_{1},p_{2},\cdots,p_{k},\lambda)}{\partial \lambda}&=\sum_{k=1}^{N}p_{k} -1 =0 \end{aligned}∂pk​∂J(p1​,p2​,⋯,pk​,λ)​∂λ∂J(p1​,p2​,⋯,pk​,λ)​​=log2​pk​+ln21​+λ=0,k=1,2,⋯,N=k=1∑N​pk​−1=0​
